

# MedTripz - Medical Tourism Website Plan

## Project Overview
A professional medical tourism platform connecting international patients (from Middle East, Africa, and CIS regions) with world-class hospitals in India. The website will feature a modern healthcare design with blue and green colors, clean layouts, and a trustworthy feel.

---

## Design System
- **Primary Color**: Deep blue (#1E40AF) - trust, professionalism
- **Secondary Color**: Teal/Green (#059669) - health, wellness
- **Accent**: Soft blue gradients for hero sections
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif fonts
- **Style**: Rounded corners, soft shadows, generous white space

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage
- **Header**: Logo (MedTripz), navigation menu (Home, Hospitals, Patient Stories, About Us, Blogs, Contact Us), language selector, phone number, and "Get Free Quote" CTA button
- **Hero Section**: Full-width with gradient overlay, headline targeting international patients, search bar, and lead capture form on the right side
- **Lead Form Fields**: Patient name, country, city, mobile, email, medical condition, date of birth, document upload (PDF/JPG/PNG)
- **Trust Section**: Key statistics and partner hospital logos
- **Services Overview**: Cards highlighting medical tourism services

### 2. Hospitals Listing Page
- **Filter Bar**: Dropdowns for Country, City, and Specialities
- **Hospital Cards Grid**: 3 columns on desktop, responsive on mobile
- **Card Elements**: Hospital image, name, location with icon, "More Details" and "Book Appointment" buttons
- **Sample hospitals**: Artemis, Fortis, Medanta, and more

### 3. Overview Landing Page (New Design)
- **Hero**: "Your Trusted Gateway to World-Class Healthcare in India"
- **What We Help With**: 6 service cards (Hospital Discovery, Second Opinion, Appointments, Visa, Travel, Follow-up)
- **How It Works**: 5-step process visualization
- **Trust Indicators**: NABH/JCI hospitals, experienced coordinators, 30+ countries served
- **CTA Section**: "Submit Your Case" prompt

### 4. Patient Stories Page
- **Testimonial Cards**: Patient name, treatment type, country, summary
- **Filter by treatment type or region
- **Video testimonials placeholder

### 5. Blogs Page
- **Category filters**: Treatments, Travel Guide, Patient Care, Cost Comparison
- **Blog post cards with featured image, title, excerpt, date
- **Pagination

### 6. About Us Page
- **Who We Are section
- **Our Mission statement
- **Why Choose Us**: Verified hospitals, dedicated coordinators, end-to-end support
- **Team section placeholder

### 7. Contact Us Page
- **Contact form**: Name, Email, Phone, Country, Message
- **Office location and contact details
- **Map placeholder

---

## Admin Dashboard
- **Secure login** for administrators
- **Hospital Management**: Add, edit, delete hospitals with images, locations, specialities
- **Lead Management**: View and manage form submissions
- **Content Management**: Blog posts and patient stories

---

## Backend & Database (Lovable Cloud)
- **Tables**: Hospitals, Leads, Patient Stories, Blog Posts, Admin Users
- **Email Integration**: Send form submissions to a single recipient email
- **File Storage**: Patient document uploads (PDFs, images)
- **Authentication**: Admin login system

---

## Form Behavior
- **Validation**: All required fields validated before submission
- **Email Notification**: Leads sent to designated email address
- **App Download Prompt**: Modal appears after form submission encouraging mobile app download (Android/iOS buttons + "Continue on Website")

---

## Mobile Responsiveness
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Mobile-optimized navigation with hamburger menu
- Touch-friendly buttons and forms
